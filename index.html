Make Bank?

Make Bank is a remake of the popular game, Make Money.

How To Play

Literally just smack the button with the $ on it.

Upgrades

Click the upgrades tab to get to the shop, then buy upgrades!

The "Income Boost" boosts your income by 10%

The "Bonus Clicks" increases the bonus clicks powerup by 0.1. Each time you click, you get a certain amount of bonus clicks. When used, converts to money!

The "Auto Click" increases the auto powerup by 2.5x. Every second, you get a set amount of times your current income.
